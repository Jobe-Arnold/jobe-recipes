Southern Pad Thai

This pad thai could not be less authentic but my friends like to eat it

Use a saucepan instead of a skillet cause the jar sauce will make your skillet smell like fish!

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless chicken thighs (cut into strips)
  • A jar of pad thai sauce
  • A bag of pad thai noodles
  • 3 eggs
  • Green onions (chopped)
  • A couple handfuls of spinach
  • Crushed peanuts
  • Lime slices
  • Seasonings
    • Red Pepper Flakes
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Onion Powder
    • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Check instructions on noodles and check if you want to use the fast or slow instructions
    • If slow, start noodles well before heating pan
    • If fast, wait to put noodles into boiling water until you add chicken to pan
  2. Heat avocado oil in a large saucepan on high heat
  3. Add red pepper flakes and stir for 1 minute
  4. Season chicken, cut it, add it to the pan, and stir until cooked (you can put the lid on to make sure it gets the inside)
  5. Move the chicken to the side and crack the eggs in the open space. You may need to add more oil
  6. Add chopped onions and scramble the eggs in the space
  7. Add spinach and mix all together
  8. Add finished noodles and the whole jar of sauce
  9. Stir until all combined
  10. Add crushed peanuts and squeeze lemon slices
  11. Enjoy!
