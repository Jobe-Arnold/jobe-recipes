Crispy Cast Iron Chicken Thighs

These chicken thighs are quick, easy, and taste great with rice and broccoli!

I could eat these every day for the rest of my life.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • Seasonings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Garlic Powder
    • Onion Powder
    • Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Pat chicken thighs dry with a paper towel and season
  3. Heat cast iron skillet with oil
  4. Place thighs in skillet and let sit until crispy (about 7-8 min)
  5. Move skillet to oven
  6. Bake in oven for 10 minutes
  7. Flip thighs in skillet
  8. Bake for another 10 minutes (until thighs reach 165F)
  9. Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes
  10. Enjoy! Pour a little leftover oil in rice for extra flavor
